Voltz

A community revitalization of the original Voltz modpack

The original Voltz modpack on the Technic Platform has long surpassed the point of modern relevance. Over the years, numerous clones have been created, but none could capture the imagination in the manner of the orginal pack. It's time for a modpack that embodies the true spirit of the orginal. It's time for a new Voltz.

The Voltz Community Modpack will be the first update of Voltz to feature every single one of the original core mods. Help us create the future of Voltz.
