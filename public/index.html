Voltz

A community revitalization of the original Voltz modpack

The original Voltz modpack on the Technic Platform has long surpassed the point of modern relevance. Over the years, numerous people have attempted to create clones including the Iguana pack and Electric Potato, but none could capture the imagination in the manner of the orginal pack. It's time for a modpack that embodies the true spirit of the orginal. It's time for Voltz.
